About the app

Record your journey with the STEP App Recorder !

You can create your personalised trip before tracking your commute. Furthermore it is automatically saved on your phone so you have to only enter it once. Then you just have to click the "Start Tracking" button and see your journey on the map in direct if you want. To stop the record just press the "stop tracking" button and send your trip to the server. Thank's for your contribution !
